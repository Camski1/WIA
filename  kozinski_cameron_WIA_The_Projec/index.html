Welcome to Human Projects,

Human Project is a non-profit charity that is hoping to bring people together and make the world a better place. At Human Projects our idea of a better place is simply that every thinking being in the universe will suffer as little as possible. We understand that life can be, and will be difficult but, meaningless struggles can be avoided. We know the goal is impossible, but we also know that every step in that direction will bring us to a better state of living. You are probably wondering how we are trying to move towards this goal. The answer is that we find project around the world with the help of people like you and just do it. Our projects have been everything from helping to combat elephant poaching, to teaching girls to read in Iraq. Please help in any way you can.
